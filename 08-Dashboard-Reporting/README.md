**Dashboard & Reporting**

**Overview**

This folder contains reporting and dashboard artifacts for the AI-Assisted Ticket Workflow Management System.

The objective is to provide stakeholders with clear visibility into operational performance, ticket management efficiency, AI effectiveness, service levels, and business outcomes through meaningful metrics and visual reporting.

**Contents**

Dashboard Reporting Template - Defines key performance indicators (KPIs), reporting requirements, visualization concepts, and stakeholder reporting needs.


**Reporting Areas**

The dashboard framework may include:

-Ticket Volume & Trends

-Ticket Resolution Performance

-SLA Compliance

-Agent Productivity

-AI Categorization Accuracy

-AI Routing Effectiveness

-AI Prediction Performance

-Customer Satisfaction Metrics

-Operational Efficiency Indicators

**Objectives**

-Enable data-driven decision making

-Monitor service and operational performance

-Measure AI effectiveness and business value

-Identify trends, bottlenecks, and improvement opportunities

-Support executive and operational reporting

**Outcome**

A well-defined reporting framework helps stakeholders monitor system performance, track business objectives, measure AI impact, and drive continuous improvement across the ticket management lifecycle.
